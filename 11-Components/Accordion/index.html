Artificial Intelligence

+

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think like humans and mimic their actions. AI can be used for various applications, from speech recognition to complex problem-solving.

Climate Change

+

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns, primarily due to human activities such as burning fossil fuels. These changes are causing more severe weather events, rising sea levels, and biodiversity loss.

Blockchain Technology

+

Blockchain technology is a decentralized, distributed ledger that records the provenance of a digital asset. Originally designed for digital currency transactions, it is now used in various industries for secure, transparent, and tamper-proof data storage.
